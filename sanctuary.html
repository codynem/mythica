smol sanctuary.


Since 2010, the government in Canmore Alberta has spent over four hundred thousand dollars euthanizing european domestic rabbits. These attempts have been mostly unsuccessful in creating a compassionate, sustainable and effective solution to the problem. To date, there are still reports of thousands of feral rabbits in the area.

We propose the construction of a large scale sanctuary facility as a proof of concept to sustainably house 200 domestic rabbits.

This relieves local governments from hundreds of thousands of tax dollars while simultaneously stimulating the local economy by creating new jobs, and standing as a halmark of canadian innovation, sustainbility, and compassion.
